## Definition 2.5.1.1 (Recursive Comprehensive Transparency Measure)

$$T^{(R)}(l,m;f_n) := 1 - \max\left\{\lambda_{l,m}^{(R)}(f_n), \beta_{l,m}^{(R)}(f_n), \frac{\gamma_{l,m}^{(R)}}{2}\right\}$$

where all indicators are based on dynamic modulation of relativistic index $\eta^{(R)}(l; m)$.

**Property 2.5.1.1**: Basic properties of recursive transparency measure

2. **Dynamic complete transparency condition**: $T^{(R)}(l,m;f_n) = 1 \Leftrightarrow \lambda^{(R)} = \beta^{(R)} = \gamma^{(R)} = 0$

## Theorem 2.5.1.1 (Transparency Grading of Recursive Coordinate Systems)

Based on recursive comprehensive transparency, establish grading of recursive coordinate systems:
